F.A.Q

  • What is TTLCC?

    TTLCC stands for Technology Transfer Licensing and Commercialization Center. It is a center that is responsible for the management and commercialisation of intellectual property and innovation outputs of the university i.e Harare Institute of Technology.

  • What is Technology Transfer?

    Is the process of transferring scientific and technological research and innovation outputs from the university to public and private industry for the purpose of further development and / or commercialization

  • What is the process if I wish to partner with HIT on developing an innovation?

    As the party expressing interest, you write an expression of interest to partner with institute. Should the expression of interest be met favorably, you progress to signing on Non Disclosure Agreement (NDA) and / or Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for the partnership to be satisfied.

  • What is an innovation hub?

    It is a science and technology park that focuses on developing innovative products, services and training in specific areas of specialization by providing a collaborative workspace vibe.

  • What is the connection between Innovation Hub and TTLCC?

    TTLCC is the custodian of the Innovation Hub.

  • What innovations has TTLCC seen through to commercialisation?

    Developing ERPs for City Councils, Town Councils and Rural District Councils.
    ZUPCO Tap-card system
    National Fuels Management system
    Smart Vehicle Tracking
    Unipay- a smart transaction platform between Universities and their various stakeholders
    The “Sahara Fuel card” - an improvement to the National Fuel Management system
